Divider Applications

PP corrugated dividers are specified wherever product-to-product contact causes damage:

  • Glass bottles and jars — cell dividers prevent breakage
  • Fresh produce — individual fruit/vegetable separation
  • Pharmaceutical — vial and bottle partitioning
  • Automotive parts — component isolation, no metal-to-metal contact
  • Electronics — anti-static grade available
  • Cosmetics — unit separation in retail display boxes

How PP Corrugated Dividers Are Made

Dividers start as flat 48×96 in PP corrugated sheet stock, then:

  • Cut to the box interior height and width
  • Slotted at regular intervals to half-depth
  • Assembled by interlocking slots — no adhesive needed
  • Cell count and size configured to product dimensions

Custom tooling available for high-volume orders. Standard sizes ship from US stock within 2–5 days.
