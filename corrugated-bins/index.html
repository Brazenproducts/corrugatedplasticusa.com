Bins vs Boxes — When to Use Each

The distinction matters for packaging decisions:

  • Bins — open-top, for loading/displaying/picking product; no lid; easy hand access
  • Boxes — closed, sealed for shipping; protects contents from contamination and damage in transit
  • PP corrugated bins are common in produce distribution, retail back-stock, and farm pack operations
  • PP corrugated boxes are used for sealed shipment where the product needs containment
